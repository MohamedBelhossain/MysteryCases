Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy a case?
You can purchase cases directly from the "Buy Cases" page by selecting the case you want and following the payment instructions.
Can I share my investigation progress with friends?
Yes, you can share your progress by sharing the unique link generated for each case.
What happens if I get stuck on a clue?
Don’t worry! You can use hints provided within each case or contact our support team for assistance.
Is my personal data safe?
Absolutely. We use the latest security standards to protect your data and never share it with third parties.
